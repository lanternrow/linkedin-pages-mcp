# Privacy Policy — Lantern Row Page Analytics

**Effective date:** June 25, 2026

Lantern Row ("we," "us," "our") operates *Lantern Row Page Analytics*, an application that helps LinkedIn Company Page administrators understand the organic performance of the Pages they manage. This policy explains what data the application accesses through LinkedIn's APIs, how we use it, and how we protect it.

## Who this applies to

This policy covers the *Lantern Row Page Analytics* application and the administrators of LinkedIn Company Pages who authorize it. We access data only for Pages whose authorized administrators have granted consent through LinkedIn's official OAuth authorization flow.

## What data we access

Through LinkedIn's Community Management API, and only after a Page administrator authorizes us, we access **aggregate Company Page analytics**, including:

- Follower counts, follower growth, and aggregate follower demographics (e.g., industry, seniority, job function, company size, and geography — in aggregate only)
- Page view and visitor statistics
- Organic post and share performance (impressions, clicks, reactions, comments, shares, engagement rate)
- The Page's profile information (name, description) and the Page's own posts

We do **not** access, collect, or store individual members' personal data — for example, the identities or profiles of people who follow, like, or comment. The application is limited to aggregate, Page-level reporting data.

## How we use the data

We use this data solely to present analytics and performance reporting to the authorized administrators of the relevant Company Page, so they can evaluate content performance and audience growth. We do **not** use LinkedIn data for advertising, lead generation, sales prospecting, audience building, or any automated decision-making about individuals.

## How we store and retain the data

- We store only aggregate Page reporting data, and only for as long as needed to show historical trends to the Page's authorized administrators.
- Consistent with LinkedIn's data storage requirements, aggregate Page reporting data is retained for no more than twelve (12) months.
- We do not store individual member-level data.
- Access tokens and credentials are stored securely and are never exposed publicly.

## How we share the data

We do **not** sell, rent, license, or share LinkedIn data with third parties. We do not combine LinkedIn data with data from other sources to build or enrich profiles of individuals. Analytics are displayed only to administrators associated with the relevant Page.

## Data deletion

Administrators may request deletion of their Page's data at any time by contacting us at info@lanternrow.com, and we will delete the relevant data promptly. We also delete stored LinkedIn data when an administrator revokes authorization, when a Page is no longer managed by us, or if our access to LinkedIn's APIs ends.

## Compliance

We operate in accordance with the LinkedIn API Terms of Use and the LinkedIn Marketing API Terms, including their restrictions on the use, storage, combination, and transfer of LinkedIn data.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated effective date on this page.
